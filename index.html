Info about the Hackathon

Join us for a thrilling hackathon experience!

Prizes for Winners

$50 Amazon Gift Card for 1st Place and $25 for 2nd Place

No Experience Necessary

Contestants don't need any experience in any particular programming language or programming in general! For contestants who don't have a team, we will create teams with a variety of experience. If you have your own team, we will leave that as is.

Location + Time

The Hackathon will occur in TMAC from 8:30 am to 4:30 pm on Saturday, January 20th

Opportunity to Develop Skills

Learn how to design, prototype, test, and present an app within a short period of time. Topic for the app will be announced on the day of.
FAQ

Common questions

What is the date of the hackathon? January 20th from 8:30 am to 4:30 pm PST
What are the prizes for the hackathon? $50 Amazon Gift Card for 1st Place and $25 for 2nd place
How can I participate in the hackathon? You can register through the link on our website and join on January 20th from 8:30 am to 4:30 pm PST
Is there an entry fee for the hackathon? No, participation is free of charge. Food will be provided, but be sure to bring snacks and water
Can I participate as an individual or do I need a team? You can participate either as an individual or as a team of up to 4 people
